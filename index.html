Assessment

What Can Be Repaired?

✓ Repairable
  • ✓Deep vertical scratches from seizure
  • ✓Aluminum transfer from melted piston
  • ✓Seizure marks and scoring
  • ✓Rust and surface corrosion
  • ✓Worn bore (sleeve structurally sound)
  • ✓Cast iron sleeve cylinders
  • ✓Plated / Nikasil cylinders
✗ Not Repairable
  • ✗Cracks or fractures in cylinder wall
  • ✗Structural damage to the sleeve
  • ✗Bore size significantly changed
  • ✗Broken ports or missing material
  • ✗Bore diameter worn beyond specification
  • ✗Piston rocking damage (excessive bore clearance)
About bore wear:
If the bore has expanded beyond the manufacturer's specification, coating alone cannot restore proper clearance. In this case, sleeve replacement is the correct solution. We will identify this from your photos during the free evaluation — before you ship anything.

FAQ

Common Questions

Can severely damaged cylinders really be repaired? +
Even if the damage looks very bad — deep scoring, aluminum transfer, full circumference scratches — we may still be able to repair it. Send us photos and we will evaluate for free within 24 hours.
Will it work on my Nikasil / plated cylinder? +
Yes. We developed a special sand-blast preparation process that allows the dry film coating to adhere to plated surfaces. This is not commonly available elsewhere.
How long will the repair last? +
When properly broken in, the repaired cylinder will last for the remaining service life of the sleeve. This is a permanent restoration, not a temporary fix.
Do I need new pistons and rings? +
We recommend using new pistons and rings after repair. OEM or high-quality aftermarket parts will give the best result during break-in.
How long does repair take? +
Repair takes 5 to 10 days at our workshop. Shipping time is additional and depends on your country.
How much does shipping cost? +
Shipping is not included in the $300 price. Return shipping is charged separately at actual cost via EMS with full tracking.
Can you accept shipments from my country? +
Due to war zones, embargoed regions, and air freight restrictions, we cannot accept from all countries. Include your country in your first message — we will confirm immediately.
Is there a warranty? +
Yes, 6 months. The coating is durable and will not peel or flake when the break-in procedure is followed correctly.
